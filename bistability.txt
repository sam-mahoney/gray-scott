== Bistability ==

--
Bi: Two 
Stability: System stays in that place without external input
--


A bistable structure can be resting in either of two states.

On/Off | 1/0 | Activated/Deactivated

A big enough push is required to move the system to its other state.


== Gray-Scott ==

The Gray-Scott system has two stable states for any local region in the grid:

State 1 (valley A): U=1, V=0. The trivial state. Feed and decay in equilibrium, no reaction.
State 2 (valley B): U partially depleted, V present. The patterned state.

State 1 allows the react to stay self contained. It doesn't continue infinitely into space.

Since this is a bistable structure the system can sit in either state but not a mix of both. 

== Mechanics ==

Creation depends on V². Removal depends on V.

1. V from the interior diffuses outward.
2. As it spreads, concentration drops (dilution).
3. At some distance from the source, V drops below the threshold where creation = removal.
4. Past that distance, V is small enough that decay overwhelms reaction.
5. V dies.
